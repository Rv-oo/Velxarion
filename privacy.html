Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Discord bot collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our services on Discord servers. By using this bot, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Our bot does not collect personal information directly from users. However, to operate its functionality, the bot may access and process the following data:

  • Server data: server ID, channel ID, user ID, username, and roles.
  • Messages and commands: the content of messages sent to the bot, especially those using prefixes/commands.
  • Activity logs: for moderation purposes (e.g., kick, ban, mute).
  • Voice information: for music features, the bot will process voice channel data (channel ID, join/leave events).

We do not store conversations or private content outside the technical and functional context of the bot.

3. Use of Information

The information collected is used only for the following purposes:

  • Providing bot features (moderation, music, AI, translation, utilities, etc.).
  • Improving performance and user experience.
  • Storing server settings (e.g., custom prefix, moderation feature configurations).
  • Maintaining security and preventing bot abuse.

We do not use your information for advertising, sales, or share it with third parties.

4. Data Storage

All stored data is minimal and only what is necessary for the bot's functionality.

  • Data is stored on secure servers and can only be accessed by the bot's official developers.
  • Log data may be stored for a certain period for performance and security analysis and will be periodically deleted.

5. User Control

As a user:

  • You can remove the bot from your server at any time.
  • Server admins can configure and delete bot settings.
  • You can contact us to request deletion of specific server data if needed.

6. Security

We are committed to protecting your data. Our security measures include:

  • Internal access restrictions to data.
  • Encryption of sensitive data when necessary.
  • Regular updates to keep the bot secure against vulnerabilities.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. In the event of significant changes, we will notify you through the official support server or public documentation. You are encouraged to review this page periodically.

8. Contact

If you have any questions or reports regarding the bot service, you can contact us through:

  • Discord: Join Discord
  • Email: Contact Me
  • Website: My Website
